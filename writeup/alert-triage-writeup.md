## Feature Prioritization

### Must-Have (P0)
- Centralized alert list with severity-based filtering
- Alert detail view with contextual information
- Manual resolution and closure actions

### Nice-to-Have (P1)
- Suggested remediation steps
- MITRE ATT&CK mapping
- Alert history and trends

### Future Enhancements (P2)
- Alert correlation
- Automated resolution for known false positives
- AI-based alert severity tuning

**Reasoning:**  
The focus is on delivering a simple and usable workflow that solves the core problem
before introducing advanced automation or intelligence.

---

## Success Metrics (KPIs)

The success of the alert triage workflow can be measured using the following metrics:

- Mean Time to Acknowledge (MTTA)
- Mean Time to Resolve (MTTR)
- Reduction in false positive alerts
- Percentage of alerts resolved within SLA
- Adoption rate of resolution actions
